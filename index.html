Upper School Professional Expectations; Structures; Policies and Procedures

-School day hours for faculty: 7:45-3:30. Please sign out in the office if you leave the building during the day.
-Attending advisory, all-school, and class meetings; being present for extra help.
-Seating and attendance taking in the Schiff Center and at class meetings.
-Professional boundaries.
-Referrals to counseling.
-Posting course description/syllabus and grading policies to Schoology.
-Using Schoology to post all assignments and to keep all grades.
-Checking the upper and all-school calendar. Sharing announcements with student government.
-Taking attendance in advisory and at the start of every class.
-Enforcing lanyard wearing.
-Making best use of the communication cycle: assigning and grading work in the first four weeks of the quarter to provide substantive feedback for the second.
**Returning work:**
-Assignments should be assigned in class and due in class.  Assignments shouldn't appear on Schoology outside of class sessions, or be due when students are away from class.
-Assignments must be posted to Schoology by 4:00 p.m. on the school day that they are assigned.
-Students should have gotten back the last test, essay, lab report, or other major category of assignment before submitting the next one.
-Communication with parents.  The value of being proactive.
-Communication with colleagues.  Getting word out as early as possible about field trips.
-Procedures for faculty absences: email Hannah and provide a lesson plan for the sub.
-Professional dress on Monday-Thursday.  Jeans Friday.
-Divisional and departmental Tuesday afternoon meetings (3:30-5:00).
-Observations and evaluations.  The evaluation year.
-Reminders about health forms to keep insurance rates low.
